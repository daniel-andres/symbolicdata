Contacting SymbolicData

The project is currently maintained by Hans-Gert Gräbe (University of Leipzig, Germany)

To contact the SymbolicData team, please visit the Web site http://www.symbolicdata.org or send an email to the maintainer.

To get information on a more regular base in2008 we started a Google group symbolicdata. You find the rare mails from the old archive at this web site.
